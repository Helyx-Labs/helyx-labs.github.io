The Helyx Project

Helyx Labs builds tools that cut through friction. Our work spans two lanes:

  • Ship-now utilities — desktop helpers and automations that make daily work delightful.
  • Deep-tech experiments — research and simulators for aerospace and materials engineering.

Translation: one tab for getting things done, another for chasing the impossible. Both get plenty of love.

What guides us
  • Make it obvious: if you need a manual, we failed.
  • Make it fast: performance is a feature, not a wish.
  • Make it kind: the tool should reduce stress, not add steps.
