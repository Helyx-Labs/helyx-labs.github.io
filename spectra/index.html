What is Spectra?

Spectra is not a browser trying to replace the web. It’s a browser designed to help users move through it with less friction.

Performance-first by design

Spectra prioritizes speed, responsiveness, and low overhead. Every feature is evaluated against performance impact before it ships.

AI as infrastructure

AI in Spectra is not a popup or a gimmick. It’s built into how users search, explore, and work.

Built for real workflows

Research, learning, multitasking, and focused browsing are first-class use cases — not afterthoughts.

HelyxAI — your AI inside the browser

Spectra ships with HelyxAI built directly into the browser experience. No extensions. No context switching.

Helyx — your AI buddy

Helyx understands context across tabs, searches, and tasks. It helps summarize, explain, draft, and guide — without interrupting flow.

Workflow-aware assistance

Instead of generic responses, HelyxAI is designed to assist based on what you’re currently doing — research, reading, or building.

Privacy-conscious design

AI features are built with restraint. No unnecessary data retention. No opaque behaviour.

Helyx Aud — sound, without the friction

Browsing isn’t just visual. Helyx Aud integrates music and audio into your workflow seamlessly.

Adaptive listening

Helyx Aud adapts playback based on your activity and mood — whether you’re reading, working, or taking a break.

YouTube Music & Spotify integration

Native integration with popular platforms for uninterrupted, context-aware listening.

Designed to stay out of the way

Controls are subtle, accessible, and never intrusive. Audio complements work — it doesn’t compete with it.

Where Spectra is headed

Spectra is actively evolving. What exists today is a foundation — not the finish line.
